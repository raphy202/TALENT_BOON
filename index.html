Welcome to Talent Boon

'Talent Boon' emerged as social venture to help the people of economically backward and underdeveloped areas of Kerala.We aim at eradicating poverty and unemployment  from our society by inspiring the skillful.We provide the platform to these skillful community to sell thier self made products.Presently the unit is set up for Ernakulam district focussing mainly Areekal,Mulamthuruthy,Pampakuda,Elanji,Arakkunam,Thommankuth,Pazhoor. The skill development involves engaging people in various skillful activities.Some of our varieties are 'food' section which presents common meal, snacks and desserts by individual homemakers or by small self help groups.'Art Gallery' section involves the unique art work,crafts small useful household varieties,paintings.'Boutique' section involves the fashionable clothes ,Wedding gowns and Jewelleries,shoes and bags made by our skillful community.'Organic farm' section involves the organically made fresh and pesticide free fruits and vegetables.'Community' section involves the common services provided in various sectors like transportation,plumbing,maintenance work ,electric work,Labouring,mehendi design.News and updates of our present unit  and major attractions of the selected district are included in the site. read more
